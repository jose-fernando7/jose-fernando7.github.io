About Fernando

The Work is
Always Personal.

Fernando is a Los Angeles-based actor and producer whose creative practice is inseparable from his identity as a Latinx, queer artist. He builds work from the inside out — stories told by and for communities that have been waiting to see themselves on screen with the full complexity they deserve.

His producing work is built around a single conviction: representation is infrastructure. Not a checkbox. Not a trend. A deliberate architectural choice in what gets made, who makes it, and how.

The Why

Stories shape culture. For too long, Latinx and queer narratives have been filtered through a lens that wasn't ours. Fernando produces work that corrects that — from development through distribution.

The How

Packaging, financing, and shepherding projects from script to screen. Building relationships with directors and writers whose voices have been overlooked. Creating the room. Holding it open.
